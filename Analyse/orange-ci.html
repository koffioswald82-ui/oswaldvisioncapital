C'est ce qui explique la résistance des marges malgré la concurrence Wave. Orange CI has a typical integrated telecom operator cost structure:high fixed costs(depreciated network infrastructure, technical teams) andlow variable costs(marginal cost of an additional subscriber near zero once the network is deployed). This explains margin resilience despite Wave competition.
